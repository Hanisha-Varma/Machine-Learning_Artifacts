Artifact 01

The Daily Brew — AI Customer Assistant

AI CHATBOT
IntroductionAn AI-powered customer service chatbot built for a small cafe, designed and tested end-to-end using the design thinking framework (Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, Test).
DescriptionA conversational assistant that answers customer questions about hours, menu and pricing, dietary accommodations, offers, loyalty program, reservations, and catering — and knows when to hand off to a human staff member instead of guessing.
ObjectiveTo design and build a working AI assistant that solves a real small-business problem: answering repetitive customer questions instantly, without requiring 24/7 staff availability.
ProcessStarted with empathy for the end user (cafe customers wanting fast answers) before writing a line of configuration. Built a structured knowledge base covering the café's operations, then wrote custom behavioral instructions defining tone and strict boundaries (e.g., never promising allergen-free food, never committing to large catering orders). Tested with real conversations, including a simulated complaint, and used the results to identify a concrete improvement (more empathetic complaint handling) for future iteration.
Tools & TechnologiesChatbase (AI agent builder), GitHub Pages (public deployment)
Value PropositionDemonstrates the ability to translate a business problem into a working AI solution — not just prompting a chatbot, but deliberately designing its knowledge, boundaries, and escalation behavior around a real user need.
Unique ValueShows applied, hands-on AI/ML capability rather than theoretical knowledge: I can scope a problem, build a working prototype, test it critically, and identify specific, grounded improvements rather than treating the first working version as finished.
RelevanceDirectly relevant to roles applying AI to business operations — the exact intersection I'm aiming for as I move toward a career connecting AI capabilities to real business problems.

🏦 Financial Services & Banking 🧠 Artificial Intelligence 🤖 Machine Learning 📊 Deep Learning
AI STRATEGY

Applying AI, ML & Deep Learning to Transform Financial Services

An industry-level analysis exploring how banks and financial institutions can responsibly adopt intelligent technologies to modernize operations, strengthen risk management, and elevate the customer experience.

This artifact presents a structured research and strategy project examining the financial services industry through an AI lens — from foundational concepts and architecture selection to a phased enterprise roadmap grounded in responsible AI principles.

Project Overview

This project investigates how Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning are reshaping Financial Services and Banking — an industry built on trust, precision, and regulation. It examines where AI creates the most measurable business value, how Machine Learning differs from Deep Learning in practical banking applications, and what a realistic path to Enterprise AI adoption looks like. The analysis is grounded in Responsible AI principles throughout, treating fairness, transparency, and governance not as an afterthought but as a core requirement for any Business Transformation effort involving customer financial data.

Objective

To research, analyze, and present a clear, executive-level framework for how financial institutions can strategically evaluate and adopt Artificial Intelligence — balancing innovation, operational efficiency, and risk — while maintaining responsible, compliant, and customer-centered practices.
